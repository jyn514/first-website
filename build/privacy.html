Why Privacy?

Some of you may be wondering why I dedicated a large part of this site to privacy resources. It seems silly after all - why should we care what the NSA is doing? That stuff is all paranoid, and more than a hassle than anything else.

I agree. We all knew when we got a new phone that it would be tracking our location, uploading our calendars and contacts, and storing data about our preferences and actions in remote servers. We accepted it as the price of living in the modern world, of having a self-updating map at the tip of our fingers, of contacting people across the world at the speed of light.

But it doesn't have to be. We don't have to accept websites tracking our location, or the time and location of all our logins and which devices we use every time we access the web.

Join me in taking back the web.
